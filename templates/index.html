Social Media Sentiment Analysis

The Modern Approach to Market Research and Analysis For Your Brand


Tell Me More

Using Modern Day Technology

By combining the power of Social Media , Natural Language Processing and Sentiment Analysis ,
we can give you a real look at what people are saying about your organization.

How It Works

1. Social Media Research

Leverage the API's of the most popular social media platforms to get tweets and posts about your organization.

2. Read Sentiment

With the cognitive power of IBM Watson's sentiments API, we can gauge the mood of each post talking about your organization and discern the positive or negative reception

3. Aggregate Data

Using the data from the previous steps, we can begin to combine the data and generate overall scores for your brand based on sets of data we've colleted. In addition, we pay attention to the useful details like the follower counts of users that commented.

4. See the Results

Finally, we present the results in a neat and easy to use interface so you can gain the most insight from the research.
